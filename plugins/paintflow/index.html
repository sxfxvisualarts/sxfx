SXFX PaintFlow

Flows

About This Plugin

SXFX PaintFlow is an organic material simulation engine that creates stunning visual effects in real-time. Switch between two distinct modes: Paint Flow for fluid, colorful paint streams with depth and dynamic lighting, and Smoke Engine for atmospheric volumetric effects with realistic movement and light scattering.

Key Features

  • Paint Flow Mode - Fluid paint streams with realistic mixing and 3D depth
  • Smoke Engine - Volumetric smoke with organic movement and light scattering
  • 10 unique seed patterns for endless variations
  • Dynamic shadows and highlights
  • Night Mode for dramatic lighting
  • Image mask support for shape control
  • Power & Threshold controls

Technical Specifications

Format FFGL 2.x
Platform Windows / macOS
Compatible Resolume Arena & Avenue
GPU OpenGL 4.1+
