About This Plugin

SXFX Beat Shaper is a geometric shape generator designed to sync with your music. Create reactive visuals that pulse and transform to the beat of your audio in real-time. Perfect for live VJ performances, music videos, and immersive installations.

Key Features

  • Beat-reactive animation engine
  • Multiple geometric shapes (triangle, square, hexagon, circle)
  • Dynamic color modes and gradients
  • Rotation and scale controls
  • Glow and blur effects
  • Multiple blend modes

Technical Specifications

Format FFGL 2.x
Platform Windows (contact for MAC)
Compatible Resolume Arena & Avenue
GPU OpenGL 4.1+
