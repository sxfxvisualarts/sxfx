SXFX Remapper

High-performance MIDI remapping utility. Map entire banks in seconds using Simple (1:1) or Combined (Shift logic) modes. Designed for speed and efficient workflow.

Download

Map entire banks in seconds

The window stays open, letting you map controls one after another.

How to use:
  1. Click LEARN.
  2. Choose Type:
    • Simple: 1:1 Cloning.
    • Combined: Shift + Control logic.
  3. Move your controls in sequence!
  4. Click DONE when finished.
Rule Types
  • Simple Mapping: Direct control (e.g., Fader 1 controls Volume).
  • Combined Mapping: Shift + Control logic (e.g., Holding "Button 1" turns "Fader 1" into Reverb Send).
Efficient Workflow
  • Advanced Learn: Convert 360/Infinite encoders into absolute faders.
  • Smart Silence: Intelligent muting of Shift buttons to prevent DAW triggers while switching layers.
  • Panic: Automatically sends "All Notes Off" when stopping engine.
